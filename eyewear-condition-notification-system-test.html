üöÄ How It Works

1. Staff Assessment Process

Staff members can assess eyewear conditions through the admin panel:

  • Select customer from dropdown
  • Enter eyewear description (e.g., "Ray-Ban Aviator")
  • Choose condition: Good, Needs Fix, Needs Replacement, or Bad
  • Add assessment date and optional next check date
  • Include notes and send notification

2. Customer Notification Flow

When staff sends an assessment:

  • Real-time Alert: Floating notification appears on customer dashboard
  • Email Backup: Detailed email sent to customer
  • WebSocket: Instant notification via WebSocket
  • Action Buttons: Customer can schedule check or view details
