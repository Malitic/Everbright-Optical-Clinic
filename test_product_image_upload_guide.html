üìã How to Upload More Images

Step 1: Access Product Management

Log in as an Admin or Staff user and navigate to the Product Gallery.

Step 2: Add or Edit Product

Click the "Add Product" button or edit an existing product to access the image upload section.

Step 3: Upload Multiple Images

In the "Product Images" section, click "Upload Images" and select multiple image files at once (up to 8 total).

Step 4: Manage Your Images

View all uploaded images in the preview grid. You can remove individual images by clicking the ‚ùå button on each image.

Step 5: Save Product

Click "Save Product" to store your product with all uploaded images in localStorage.

üí° Pro Tips for Better Eyewear Images

  • Front View: Show the complete frame from the front
  • Side View: Display the profile and temple design
  • Detail Shots: Close-ups of hinges, logos, or special features
  • Lens View: Show the lens color and tint
  • Case/Box: Include the packaging if available
  • Size Reference: Show the glasses next to common objects
  • Different Angles: 3/4 view, top view, bottom view
  • Lifestyle: Model wearing the eyewear (if possible)
