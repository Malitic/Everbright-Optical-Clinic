üéØ How to View Reserved Products

  1. Open the Application: Go to http://localhost:5173
  2. Login: Use your admin, staff, or optometrist credentials
  3. Navigate to Patient Transactions: Click on the "Patient Transactions" tab
  4. View Patient List: See all patients with completed transactions
  5. Click Patient Name: Click on any patient name to open their details
  6. View Reserved Products: See the "Reserved Products from Gallery" section with product images and details
  7. View Transaction History: Scroll down to see detailed transaction information
